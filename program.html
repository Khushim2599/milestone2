Program Overview

Why Choose IT?

Information Technology (IT) can be found in almost every aspects of our lives: the workplace, our homes, our communication devices and even our entertainment.
Information Technology is one of the fastest growing professions. IT offers a diverse set of career paths and the ability to work from anywhere in the world! Here
at Kennesaw State, our flexible Information Technology program offers the classes that fit your career goals and your busy life by offering degree programs completely online!

Some fields you can work in as an IT professional:

Profession Median Pay Job Growth Outlook What You'll Do
Network Architect $98,400 9% Design and build data communication networks
Systems Analyst $82,700 21% Design systems solutions to help organizations run smoothly
Database Administrator $80,200 11% Use specialized software to sort and organize data
Security Analyst $88,800 18% Design solutions to protect computer networks and systems
